# Corporate-Data-Insights-Company-Performance-Workplace-Culture-Analysis
Tools- Python,SQL,PowerBI
## Project Overview
This end-to-end business intelligence project provides a comprehensive data-driven analysis of over 9,000 corporate entities spanning multiple global industries (such as IT Services, Engineering, Automotive, and Healthcare).
By analyzing large-scale datasets containing 4 million total reviewers, this project uncovers actionable insights regarding overall industry performance, company age trends, rating criteria quadrants, and employee sentiment regarding workplace culture flaws.
The final deliverable is an interactive, highly tailored Power BI executive dashboard designed to blend into a custom modern slate-and-blue corporate theme.

## Key Insights & Dashboard ArchitectureKPI Top Banner:
Displays aggregate high-level metrics including Total Company Volume ($9\text{K}$), Overall Average Rating ($3.90$), Total Reviewers ($4\text{M}$), and Average Corporate Age ($39.46\text{ years}$).Industry Performance Analysis: A ranked clustered horizontal bar chart tracking company distributions across core industry verticals, highlighting sector footprints.
Age Segments vs. Performance: A line chart identifying a clear correlation between corporate maturity and employee satisfaction, contrasting agile Startups ($0\text{-}5\text{ years}$) with Legacy organizations ($30+\text{ years}$).High-Performer Quadrant: An advanced scatter plot mapping Average Ratings across specialized categories to isolate elite operational outliers.
Workplace Culture Flaws Breakdown: A comprehensive donut chart breaking down critical employee pain points (e.g., Promotions/Growth, Salary & Benefits, Job Security) to monitor organizational risks.Top Company Matrix Table: A clean categorical grid presenting nested distributions of total companies and average performance metrics grouped by corporate lifespan phases.
